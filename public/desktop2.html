THE BUILDING

The Building

At the crossroads of three Manhattan neighborhoods, Village Green West inhabits a place where the aesthetic of the past meets the efficiency of the future. With details and finishes that are a nod to the rich context of time and place, and systems, services and amenities that are state-of-the-art in terms of comfort and sustainability, Village Green West embodies the art of living better.

RESIDENCES

Residences

Interiors are designed to engage the senses, by juxtaposing the familiar with the unique. Wide plank European oak flooring, custom paneled doors, crown moldings throughout, and black steel framed windows give the home a turn of the century warmth. Zone controlled central heating and cooling, Traco U-Value windows, and Environmental Tobacco Smoke control ensure that your home remains a sanctuary from any disturbance from the outside world.

PENTHOUSES

Penthouses

Enjoy the exclusivity of your own private floor with a spectacular 50-foot living room and unstoppable light and views to the South and North from giant floor-to-ceiling windows. Entertain or relax on one of your terraces, cook with your friends and family in your spacious custom kitchen, or just enjoy a bottle of your finest from the wine fridge.

AMENITIES

Amenities

At Village Green West, residents will take sanctuary in the Wellness Center- a first class amenity suite where fitness, relaxation, service and entertainment are available 24 hours a day. Play a game of pool in the lounge, go for a run in the gym, decompress in the sauna or have our Wellness Concierge book you a massage in the treatment room. Whatever you need to recharge, you’ll never have to leave home to find it.

NEIGHBORHOOD

One Building, Three Neighborhoods

Originally known for its bohemian culture, the West Village today is a mecca for high-end retail and acclaimed restaurants while steps away, Chelsea and the Meatpacking District have transitioned from their industrial past to become home to Manhattan’s hottest galleries, shopping, and nightlife. Village Green West could not be better situated in terms of comfort, convenience, and culture. Whether shopping on Bleecker Street,strolling on the Highline, or gallery hopping in Chelsea on opening night, the best of New York City is always just steps from your front door.

TEAM

Team


Composed of longtime residents of the downtown Manhattan area, the Alfa Development team has firsthand knowledge of the downtown experience. Always mindful of how their buildings will become part of the fabric of their community, Alfa’s guiding principle is best articulated by the theme line: “History, architecture, and sustainability on a human scale.” With an aesthetic attuned to the unique harmonies of each neighborhood, their buildings combine architecturally contextual exteriors with the most finely appointed modern interiors.

Michael Namer has been developing real estate in downtown New York for the last 30 years. As the founder, principal, and Managing Member of Alfa Development/Management LLC, Michael brings his attention to detail, construction expertise, and hands-on approach to real estate development to every project. In the last five years, Alfa Development has produced over $150,000,000 of residential condominium development — all in downtown Manhattan. Michael Namer and Alfa Development are continuing with their mission to create more sustainable living experiences with the developments and residences that comprise the Green Collection.
